Preliminary list of rules (more to be added later):
1. Each person must have the length of time of each shift off before AND after each shift prior to coming into work. So if a shift is 9 hours long, they must have 9 hours before the shift in which they are not working, and 9 hours after in which they are not working.
2. Residents may swap within different types of shifts with a certain set of rules. Senior Docs can only switch with senior docs (of either UW or H Medi), and junior docs can only switch with junior docs (similarly of either UW or H Medi). Trauma docs can switch with Senior Docs, and trauma team can switch within trauma team.
3. There cannot be swaps within levels
4. Non-dyad residents can only swap with non-dyad residents
5. They may swap between hospitals.
6. User will provide which shift they want to swap out of. Then, it will provide user with a list of shifts (with corresponding date and individual working that shift) that they can swap with - within the constraints provided.
7. The shift options must only be show up as an option if the person is a. not working at the same time b. and is not working X number of hours after their last shift and X number of hours before their next shift (X = length of their shift.
8. There is one additional shift type called Risk. These shifts are scheduled from 6am-6am the following morning. These shifts cannot be moved. But these must be taken into consideration when providing available shift options as a person cannot work a shift while they are scheduled for risk.
9. Shifts to swap cannot be at the same time as the original shift
